About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a centralized college event management platform that connects students, student coordinators, and faculty coordinators in one place. It makes event discovery, event approval, and participation tracking simple and transparent.

Students can explore upcoming events, save favorites to wishlist, and register easily. Student coordinators can create and manage event requests, while faculty coordinators review and approve events to ensure quality and relevance.

Our goal is to improve campus engagement by providing a smooth, organized, and user-friendly experience for all event-related activities.
