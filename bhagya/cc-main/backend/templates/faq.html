Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find quick answers about using CampusConnect.

1. How do I register for an event?

Go to your dashboard, open the event card, and click Register Now. Your registration will be saved to your account.

2. How can I add events to wishlist?

Use the heart (❤️) icon on any event card. You can view all saved events from the Wishlist section.

3. Why can’t I see some events?

Only approved events are visible to students. Pending or denied events are hidden until approved by the faculty coordinator.

4. I forgot my account details. What should I do?

Please contact your coordinator/admin with your registered email or student number to recover your account access.
